Sidai Cab Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Sidai Cab collects, uses, and shares information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and location information necessary for booking and providing rides.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to process bookings, improve services, and for safety purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with drivers, payment partners, and authorities when required by law.

4. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting support.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please email us at support@sidaicab.com.
